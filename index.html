A bit about me

We all have so many aspects of ourselves across a wide array of passions and interests. Real estate surprises me every day with how much of myself I get to pour into it. On any given day I get to be an advisor, an ear to listen, a data analyst, a marketer, a DIYer, a negotiator, and a friend.

I’m fortunate to be able to draw from a diverse background of experiences to serve my clients. It would be a privilege to walk through this journey with you!

Buying

Buying a home can feel daunting, especially in times when buyers are faced with a strong seller’s market – but that’s why I’m here. I thrive in highly competitive situations and I fight to get the home that is right for my clients. I’ll help you make decisions that you are confident in and that are right for you.

Selling

It’s easy when we’re in a seller’s market to cut corners, put a sign in your yard, and hope your home will sell – but I believe anything worth doing is worth doing right. I know what it takes to meticulously prepare your home for the market and put you in a position to maximize your investment. It’s not just about selling, but selling right.

Buying + Selling

If you need to sell your home while also buying your next home I am here to help. These scenarios can be complex but it doesn’t have to be stressful. It just takes careful preparation and planning. Everyone’s situations are a little bit different and they each take their own unique plan to figure out the right tactic for you.

Investing

Buying real estate as an investment is a different ballgame than buying a home for yourself. I’ll strategize with you and work to determine the viability of properties to see what works for your specific needs and goals.

I'd love to help

No matter where you are in the journey, I want to talk to you and figure out a plan fitted to your needs. Let’s talk!
